Health Awareness

Common Harmful Chemicals Found in Traditional Cleaners

Understanding these chemicals helps you make safer and healthier cleaning choices.

Ammonia

Can irritate eyes, skin, and lungs. Common in glass cleaners.

Chlorine Bleach

Releases toxic fumes when mixed with other chemicals.

Phthalates

Found in fragrances and linked to hormonal disruption.

VOCs

Cause headaches, dizziness, and long-term air pollution issues.
